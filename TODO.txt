4. Implement rate limiting 
POST /order (single order):                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
  - Burst: 3,500 req / 10s                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   
  - Sustained: 36,000 req / 10 min                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
  POST /orders (batch orders):                              
  - Burst: 1,000 req / 10s
  - Sustained: 15,000 req / 10 min

  Key details:
  - Trading endpoints enforce dual limits (burst + sustained)
  - Requests are throttled (delayed/queued), not immediately rejected
  - Uses sliding time windows
  - Batch orders: max 15 orders per request

  These limits are very generous — effectively ~350 single orders/sec burst. Unlikely to be a concern unless you're doing high-frequency trading at extreme volume.
